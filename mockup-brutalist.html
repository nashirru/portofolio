The Manifesto

The web has become a sea of sameness. Soft gradients, rounded corners, and predictable layouts generated by machines, for machines.

I believe in structural integrity over superficial polish. A website is a tool, not a painting. Every pixel should earn its place.

My code is semantic, my performance budgets are strict, and my designs are engineered to demand attention — not beg for it.

No templates. No trends. Just engineering.
